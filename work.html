I've been fortunate to do a lot of different things in journalism, working in small-town newsrooms and national bureaus. Here's a few examples of things that I've done in the past.

Story production

While working at The Register-Guard I've been able to work with reporters and photographers in producing some special online presentations. Here are a few.

Reporter Christian Hill deconstructs the heartbreaking Eugene police response that ended in the death of a wounded veteran with PTSD. I had a mountain of interviews, crime scene images, video and copy to try and piece together an engaging online experience that stepped the reader through the hour. Read the story.
A compilation of breaking news and follow-up stories about the 2014 Springfield mill fire that shook the community. The initial story was created the day after the fire and added to over time. Read the story.
On the 20th annivarsary of Kenny Wheaton's infamous "pick" I deconstructed the Jerry Allen radio call using gifs along with a story from the sports staff. See it here.
